About Us

Lumiwrks is a dynamic team established in 2023 in Indonesia, comprising three divisions: Lumiwrks Recordings, Lumiwrks Productions, and Lumiwrks Managements. Our goal is to redefine the music industry by nurturing creativity and empowering artists. We aim to make a lasting impact and create a vibrant community that celebrates diversity and innovation.

Lumiwrks Recordings is our record label, specializing in electronic music. We curate a diverse music catalog, showcasing artists who push creative boundaries and resonate with listeners on a profound level. Our platform amplifies their voices and helps them connect with a broader audience.

Lumiwrks Productions is dedicated to filmmaking and visual storytelling. We combine music and film to create immersive and engaging experiences that leave a lasting impression on audiences. Our goal is to enhance the music experience and create captivating narratives.

Lumiwrks Managements offers artist management services, providing guidance, support, and strategic planning to talented artists. We work closely with them to navigate the ever-changing music industry, foster their growth, and ensure their long-term success.

At Lumiwrks, we respect the individuality of every artist and create a collaborative environment where they feel empowered and in control of their artistic direction. While we encourage artists to release their music through Lumiwrks Recordings, we offer flexibility and understanding, allowing them to choose projects that align with their goals.

Join our vibrant community, where creativity thrives, collaboration flourishes, and innovation shines. Together, let's make a lasting impact and shape the future of the music industry.

Lumiwrks is a team founded in 2023 in Indonesia that separated into three pieces; Lumiwrks Recordings, Lumiwrks Productions, and Lumiwrks Managements. All of it is a Lumiwrks' divisions and they do not have their own websites unless stated. Started as now-inactive Lifelight Records (LFRC), this platform want a change—a change that is different and stand out from this industry.

Through Lumiwrks Recordings, the team focuses on releasing music that pushes boundaries and challenges traditional genres. Lumiwrks Productions is dedicated to creating captivating visual content, such as music videos and short films, that complement the artistic vision of the music releases. Lumiwrks Managements handles artist representation and offers comprehensive support to ensure the success and growth of the talent associated with the brand.

As Lumiwrks continues to grow and evolve, it aims to leave a lasting impact on the music landscape, establishing itself as a trailblazer in the industry. The team is committed to pushing boundaries, embracing innovation, and creating a community that celebrates artistic expression and individuality.
