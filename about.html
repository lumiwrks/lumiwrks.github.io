About.  


    Lumiwrks is an independent filmmaker from Indonesia. They specialize in creating short-form videos, particularly for lumiscape as music videos, with more exciting projects on the horizon. Their unique style often incorporates elements of "old tape" aesthetics, resulting in captivating visuals that go beyond traditional music videos.

    As a "budget" filmmaker, Lumiwrks manages to stand out from the crowd, showcasing their exceptional creativity and resourcefulness. They haven't had a project published to the public on this date, but keep on eye on them and don't miss anything from them!


"We are ready to do everything—and magic!"
